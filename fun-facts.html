Fun Facts about Dodo birds

Did you know Kevin from the movie UP was a dodo bird??
Now you do

1. Extinct but famous:
Unfortunately, the Dodo bird is no longer with us. They went extinct in the late 1600s. It would have been cool to have Dodo birds instead of pigeons in NYC

2. Flightless:
Dodos couldn’t fly. They lived on the island of Mauritius, where they had no natural predators—until humans arrived.

3. Related to Pigeons:
Believe it or not, the dodo is closely related to modern pigeons and doves.

4. Poor Survival Skills:
Since they evolved without predators, they were unafraid of humans and easy to hunt.

5. Name Origins:
The name "dodo" may have come from the Portuguese word “doudo,” meaning foolish or simple.
